Welcome to my personal archive of essays and reflections. This English section is part of an evolving multilingual project that bridges experience, language, and conscience.

Some of the writings gathered here emerged from the quiet urgency of lived moments — from the war zones of West Africa and the Levant, to the muted grief of exile, to the corridors of international cooperation. Others are meditations shaped by years of silent observation and ethical inquiry, touching on diplomacy, displacement, literature, and the quiet power of language.

These texts are not declarations, but offerings. They are addressed to those who still seek clarity amidst noise, and who sense that the spoken and the unwritten are bound together in the fragile geometry of meaning.
